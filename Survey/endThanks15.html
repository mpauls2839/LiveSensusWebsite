More information on how this data will be used can be found below.

Who We Are: We are a group of undergraduate students working within the Alchemy Technology Foundry from the Department of ECE at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

What will this survey data be used for: We are trying to assess how people perceive audio and video quality when watching live streams and participating in video conferencing calls. Live streaming and video conferencing interactions have become more relevant than ever before, and we think the quality of these live streams is a particularly relevant issue. Our ultimate goal is to be able to use this data as a starting point to make live streaming and video conferencing a much more enjoyable and seamless experience.
